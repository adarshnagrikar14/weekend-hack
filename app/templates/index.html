FAQs

How long does access last?

Access tokens expire after 1 day. Sign in again when it expires.

Do sessions renew automatically?

No. When access expires, sign in again to continue.

What can each role do?

Admin/manager can view stats; analyst can run pipeline; viewer can inspect outcomes.

Are notifications sent automatically?

No. Notification content is generated as drafts for review.
